== Installation ==

1. Once activated, click the "Price Tables" link in your left navigation panel
2. Edit the settings to your preference.
3. Test it out in a post or page.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I've installed the plugin, what now? =

Once installed and activated, a link should appear in your left navigation panel within your WP-ADMIN section. Click on the "Price Tables" link and follow the on screen instructions.
